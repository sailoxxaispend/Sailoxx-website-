FAQ

Common questions

Do you need access to our internal systems?
+
No. We never need login credentials or system access. We work from a tool inventory you fill in, software invoices you share, and an anonymous employee survey your team fills at their own time. All data handling is confidential and covered by an NDA before we start.
How much time does this take from our team?
+
Minimal. The manager filling the tool inventory sheet needs about 20–30 minutes. Individual team members filling the survey need 5 minutes each. The only calls required are one 20-minute kickoff and one 30-minute debrief. Total time from your team is under 2 hours across 5 days.
What if we only have 3–4 AI tools? Is it still worth it?
+
Sometimes the most valuable finding is that 3 tools are doing the job of 1. And sometimes we find that a single tool is costing ₹50,000/month with only 2 of the 20 licenses actively used. Even small tool stacks often have significant waste. We'll tell you on the discovery call if the math doesn't justify an audit for your specific situation.
Is the survey really anonymous for employees?
+
Yes. The survey is a Google Form with no name field required. We never share individual responses with your management team. We only present aggregated findings in the report. Employees can be honest about what isn't working without fear of attribution.
What if the audit finds we're already spending efficiently?
+
Then you have something extremely valuable — documented proof that your AI spend is justified and optimised. You can show that report to your board, your CFO, or your investors. That certainty has real value. In our experience, though, every company we've audited has found at least one meaningful saving — even well-run ones.
Can you help us implement the recommendations?
+
Yes. Our monthly monitoring retainer includes implementation follow-up — we track whether the changes in the roadmap are happening, flag if anything is drifting, and advise on any new tools your team wants to adopt. Think of it as an ongoing AI spend accountability partner.
